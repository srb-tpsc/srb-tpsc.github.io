Who We Are

Brotherhood Through Service

The Scouts Royale Brotherhood (SRB) stands as one of the Philippines' most distinguished international service fraternities and sororities. Founded in 1975, the Brotherhood has built an enduring legacy rooted in community service, principled leadership, and the unbreakable bonds of brotherhood across the nation.

The Tarlac Provincial Supreme Council proudly leads the Brotherhood's mission within the province — organizing outreach programs, civic engagements, and community-driven initiatives that uplift families and empower individuals across Tarlac.

Our members carry the 3 Guiding Principles: Leadership, Friendship, and Service — a legacy dedicated to creating tomorrow's leaders through meaningful community service today.
